MVP Framework

Core Features (Phase 1)

1. Research Assistant

AI-powered paper discovery, summarization, and citation extraction. Upload PDFs or search academic databases to find relevant research instantly.

2. AI Learning Companion

Personal AI tutor that explains complex research concepts, answers questions, and provides learning guidance tailored to your research topic.

3. Research Study Tools

Structured tools for conducting research studies: hypothesis testing, data collection templates, statistical analysis guides, and study design frameworks.

4. Citation Management

Generate citations in APA, MLA, Chicago, Harvard, IEEE, BibTeX formats. One-click export to Word, Google Docs, Zotero, Mendeley.

5. Research Paper Library

Cloud-based storage for PDFs with full-text search, tagging, annotation, and collaborative sharing capabilities.

6. Study Progress Tracking

Track research progress, set milestones, monitor reading completion, and visualize your research journey with interactive dashboards.
